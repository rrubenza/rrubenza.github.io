Welcome

I use the radial velocity (RV) technique to understand the demographics of exoplanets in our galaxy. I'm particularly motivated to advance RV capabilities to be sensitive to Earth-Sun analogs. Towards this, I built the "Solar Calibrator" to study stellar activity up-close on our nearest Sun-like star.
